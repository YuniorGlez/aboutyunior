What I love

Well, I am a multidisciplinary guy and this is a problem to select only one thing that i like, because I have the capacity to do fast and in an efficient way a lot of things and i don’t have problem to do it. But, in this 4 years of entrepreneurship and development, I’ve noticed that the thing I do better and the thing that I can make during long time excited and I love, is the process to create the idea to solve the problem.

I love to participate and lead the brainstorming process, thinking in features, thinking in the medicine that the customer need to solve her headache. Stay in the customer’s brain and find what is the main problem that the customer has, or what is the principle feature that this product has to have to solve the real problem.

Think in the MVP and the main solution which we have to put all our effort and not to expend time and money in other solutions and features. And then, look for the pieces of the puzzle that allow us to reach the solution we are looking for in the most efficient way and making the customer happy

This is what I really love. Design the product and create the ideas, this love made me to participate in more than 5 hackathons and be the mentor of the last events, to live these moments of creation
